Covid Memories Manager Demo

The COVID Memories Project is a curated collection of personal narratives and reflections from the early days of the pandemic. Our Covid Memories Manager tool allows users to manage, add, and remove audio files with playback functionality, without the need of Alexa or other smart speaker devices. This project connects to Google Sheets to provide back-end management of content for those with little to no programming experience. This Covid Memories Demo is currently under construction by undergraduate students from Western Washington University's computer science department.

To start your own audio collection, fill out the Add Content form below to aggregate new content. All audio files will need to be uploaded to the user's own Google Drive and supplied in the URL field below in mp3 format. Once content is uploaded, it will be visible in the Playlist Content table.
